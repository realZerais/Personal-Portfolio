    ABOUT ME

    Hi, I am Dave Jamil D. Siarez. I discovered coding when and my best friend in high school downloaded an AI app for fun out of boredom in class. The AI app won't do the commands that we wanted it to execute so, we tried to decipher how it is made. That's the time that I discovered this thing called "programming languages". Since then, I have been curious and fascinated about software development and how it is made, which sparked my passion for it.


    I aspire to create meaningful software that will be useful for everyone and will positively affect our lives. Through that, I can have a sense of purpose in this world.
